Powering the Future of Texas

Our mission is to develop, finance, and operate a portfolio of clean energy assets, beginning with solar and evolving to advanced nuclear, enhancing grid reliability and enabling new frontiers.

The ERCOT Opportunity

Texas's deregulated, "energy-only" market fosters intense competition and high-reward opportunities, with wholesale prices able to reach $5,000/MWh during peak demand.

Phased Execution

A multi-phased strategy mitigates risk by building from utility-scale solar to C&I projects, creating a stable financial foundation for long-term growth and innovation.

The Nuclear Frontier

Our long-term vision is to pioneer the commercialization of micro-nuclear reactors, addressing ERCOT's reliability needs and developing technology for space exploration.

Phase 1: Utility-Scale Solar

This phase establishes our market position by developing a 100-acre solar farm to sell power directly into the ERCOT wholesale market. Success in this phase is about mastering the complex interconnection process and building a track record of execution.

Phases 2 & 3: Commercial & Industrial (C&I)

Here, we diversify our revenue by becoming a distributed energy services provider. We'll develop solar projects on warehouse rooftops and parking lots, selling cheaper, clean power directly to businesses.

Model 1: Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)

A "solar-as-a-service" model. We install, own, and operate the system at no upfront cost to the client. They sign a long-term contract to buy the power produced at a rate lower than their utility.

  • ✓ Reduced electricity bills for host
  • ✓ $0 upfront cost for host
  • ✓ 15-25 year term

Model 2: Rooftop / Land Lease

A simpler model where we lease the rooftop or parking lot space. We install and own the system, selling all power to the grid. The property owner receives a stable, fixed lease payment.

  • ✓ Fixed lease income for host
  • ✓ Monetizes unused space
  • ✓ 20-30 year term
